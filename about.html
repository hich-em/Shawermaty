ABOUT US

Craving for a Mouthwatering Shawerma???

Crave no more because Shawermaty Gourmet is here to serve you the best Shawerma in town. With more than 50 years of extensive Shawerma experience, Shawermaty Gourmet team have created the best mix of authentic spices, mixed with the highest quality of beef & chicken to craft for you specialty Shawerma Sandwiches & meals.
Shawermaty Gourmet is a dedicated Shawerma joint opened in JLT, Dubai with a vision to become The No.1 brand in UAE, GCC & the region for fast food Shawerma specialty restaurants that exceeds all expectations. The taste is backed up with a special in house R&D team, which in fact uses only the highest quality ingredients available in the market.
Our main focus is to please our clients with the best mix of Taste, Quality, Innovation & Creativity, On Time Delivery, & The Traditional Authentic Arabic Hospitality.

Vision

To become the leading and most desirable Gourmet Shawerma delivery brand in the UAE, and to spread our brand through franchising across the GCC

Mission

To provide Shawerma lovers in the UAE & GCC with high quality gourmet Shawerma to their door steps as efficiently & economically as possible
